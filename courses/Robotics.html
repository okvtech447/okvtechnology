Robotics Course

Dive into the fascinating world of robotics and shape the future of automation

Course Overview

Our comprehensive Robotics course is designed to take you on an exciting journey through the rapidly evolving field of robotics. From the fundamental principles to cutting-edge applications, this course covers a wide range of topics to prepare you for a career in robotics engineering and research.

You'll gain hands-on experience with robot design, programming, and control systems. Our state-of-the-art robotics lab provides you with access to various robotic platforms, sensors, and actuators, allowing you to apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.

Whether you're a beginner with a passion for robots or an experienced engineer looking to specialize in robotics, this course will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to innovate in this dynamic field.

Duration: 16 weeks
Skill Level: Intermediate to Advanced
Prerequisites: Basic programming knowledge, fundamentals of physics and mathematics
Certificate: OKV Technology Robotics Specialist Certification

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Robotics

  • History and evolution of robotics
  • Types of robots and their applications
  • Basic components of a robotic system
  • Introduction to robot kinematics and dynamics

Module 2: Robot Design and Mechanics

  • Mechanical design principles for robots
  • 3D modeling and simulation of robotic systems
  • Actuators, sensors, and control systems
  • Robot end-effectors and manipulation

Module 3: Robot Programming and Control

  • Programming languages for robotics (Python, C++, ROS)
  • Motion planning and trajectory generation
  • Feedback control systems
  • Real-time operating systems for robots

Module 4: Computer Vision and Perception

  • Image processing and feature extraction
  • 3D perception and depth sensing
  • Object detection and recognition
  • Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM)

Module 5: Artificial Intelligence in Robotics

  • Machine learning for robot control
  • Reinforcement learning in robotics
  • Natural language processing for human-robot interaction
  • Swarm robotics and multi-agent systems

Module 6: Advanced Topics and Applications

  • Industrial robotics and automation
  • Autonomous vehicles and drones
  • Medical and assistive robotics
  • Soft robotics and bio-inspired designs
