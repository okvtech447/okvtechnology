UI/UX Design Course

Master the art of creating intuitive and engaging user experiences

Course Overview

Our UI/UX Design course is crafted to transform you into a skilled designer capable of creating user-centered digital experiences. This comprehensive program covers all aspects of user interface and user experience design, from foundational principles to advanced techniques used in today's dynamic digital landscape.

Throughout this course, you'll dive deep into user research, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. You'll gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and learn how to apply design thinking to solve real-world problems. Whether you're a beginner aspiring to start a career in UI/UX design or a professional looking to enhance your skills, this course will equip you with the knowledge and practical abilities to create compelling digital products that users love.

Duration 14 weeks
Skill Level Beginner to Intermediate
Prerequisites Basic computer skills and an eye for design
Certificate OKV Technology UI/UX Designer Certification

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to UI/UX Design

  • Understanding the role of UI/UX in product development
  • Design thinking and user-centered design principles
  • Psychology of user behavior and decision making
  • Current trends and future of UI/UX design

Module 2: User Research and Analysis

  • User research methods and techniques
  • Creating user personas and journey maps
  • Conducting user interviews and surveys
  • Analyzing and synthesizing research data

Module 3: Information Architecture and Wireframing

  • Principles of information architecture
  • Creating site maps and user flows
  • Wireframing techniques and best practices
  • Introduction to popular wireframing tools

Module 4: UI Design and Prototyping

  • Visual design principles for digital interfaces
  • Color theory, typography, and iconography in UI design
  • Creating high-fidelity mockups and prototypes
  • Hands-on practice with industry-standard design tools

Module 5: Usability Testing and Iteration

  • Planning and conducting usability tests
  • Analyzing test results and prioritizing improvements
  • Iterative design process and continuous improvement
  • Measuring and optimizing user experience
