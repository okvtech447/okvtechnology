Sales and Negotiation Course

Master the art of closing deals and negotiating in the tech industry

Course Overview

Our Sales and Negotiation course is designed to equip you with the essential skills needed to excel in the competitive world of tech sales. This comprehensive program covers modern sales techniques, negotiation strategies, and the unique aspects of selling technology products and services.

Throughout this course, you'll learn how to identify customer needs, create compelling value propositions, and close deals effectively. You'll also develop advanced negotiation skills that will help you navigate complex business situations and achieve win-win outcomes. Whether you're new to sales or an experienced professional looking to enhance your skills in the tech industry, this course will provide you with the tools and knowledge to succeed.

Duration 8 weeks
Skill Level Beginner to Intermediate
Prerequisites Basic understanding of business concepts
Certificate OKV Technology Sales and Negotiation Specialist Certification

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Foundations of Tech Sales

  • Understanding the tech sales landscape
  • Buyer personas and decision-making processes in tech
  • The sales funnel and customer journey in B2B tech sales
  • Ethics and compliance in tech sales

Module 2: Effective Sales Techniques

  • Prospecting and lead generation in the digital age
  • Crafting compelling sales pitches for tech products
  • Consultative selling and solution-based approaches
  • Handling objections and overcoming resistance

Module 3: Negotiation Fundamentals

  • Key principles of successful negotiations
  • Understanding negotiation styles and when to use them
  • Preparing for negotiations: research and strategy
  • Building rapport and trust in negotiations

Module 4: Advanced Negotiation Tactics

  • Power dynamics and leverage in tech negotiations
  • Negotiating complex deals and contracts
  • Cross-cultural negotiations in the global tech market
  • Closing techniques and sealing the deal

Module 5: Technology Sales Tools and CRM

  • Leveraging CRM systems for sales success
  • Sales automation and AI-powered tools
  • Data analytics for sales forecasting and performance
  • Social selling and digital networking strategies
