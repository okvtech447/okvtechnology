Graphics Design Course

Unleash your creativity and master industry-standard design tools

Duration

8 weeks

Level

Beginner to Intermediate

Prerequisites

Basic computer skills, creativity, and passion for design

Course Description

Our Graphics Design course is tailored to help you develop the skills needed to create stunning visual content for various mediums. From branding and marketing materials to digital illustrations and user interfaces, you'll learn to bring your creative visions to life using industry-standard tools and techniques. This course combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice, ensuring you're ready to tackle real-world design challenges.

Course Syllabus

Week 1-2: Introduction to Graphic Design

  • Design principles and elements
  • Color theory and psychology
  • Typography fundamentals
  • Introduction to Adobe Creative Suite

Week 3-4: Digital Illustration and Vector Graphics

  • Adobe Illustrator basics and advanced techniques
  • Creating logos and icons
  • Vectorizing raster images
  • Designing infographics

Week 5-6: Photo Editing and Manipulation

  • Adobe Photoshop essentials
  • Photo retouching and restoration
  • Creating photo compositions
  • Digital painting techniques

Week 7: Layout Design and Print Production

  • Adobe InDesign basics
  • Designing brochures, flyers, and posters
  • Magazine and book layout
  • Preparing files for print

Week 8: Digital Design and Portfolio Development

  • Designing for web and mobile interfaces
  • Creating social media graphics
  • Building a professional design portfolio
  • Final project presentation
