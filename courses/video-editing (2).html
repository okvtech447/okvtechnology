Video Editing Course

Master the art of storytelling through video

Duration

10 weeks

Level

Beginner to Intermediate

Prerequisites

Basic computer skills and a passion for visual storytelling

Course Description

Our Video Editing course is designed to take you from a complete beginner to a confident video editor. You'll learn industry-standard software, techniques for crafting compelling narratives, and how to create visually stunning videos for various platforms. Whether you're looking to start a career in video production or enhance your content creation skills, this course will provide you with the knowledge and hands-on experience you need to succeed.

Course Syllabus

Week 1-2: Introduction to Video Editing

  • Understanding video formats and codecs
  • Introduction to editing software (Adobe Premiere Pro)
  • Basic editing techniques: cuts, transitions, and trimming
  • Organizing and managing your footage

Week 3-4: Advanced Editing Techniques

  • Color correction and grading
  • Working with audio: cleaning, mixing, and adding music
  • Creating titles and lower thirds
  • Keyframing and basic motion graphics

Week 5-6: Storytelling Through Editing

  • Narrative structure and pacing
  • Montage and parallel editing techniques
  • Creating emotion through editing choices
  • Working with dialogue and interview footage

Week 7-8: Visual Effects and Compositing

  • Introduction to Adobe After Effects
  • Creating simple animations and titles
  • Chroma keying and green screen techniques
  • Tracking and stabilization

Week 9-10: Final Projects and Workflow Optimization

  • Creating a short film or promotional video
  • Optimizing your editing workflow
  • Exporting for different platforms (web, mobile, broadcast)
  • Building your editing portfolio
