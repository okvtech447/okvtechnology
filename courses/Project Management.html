Project Management Course

Master the art of leading successful projects in the tech industry

Course Overview

Our Project Management course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to successfully lead and manage projects in the fast-paced tech industry. This comprehensive program covers traditional and agile project management methodologies, with a focus on their application in technology-driven environments.

Throughout this course, you'll learn how to initiate, plan, execute, monitor, and close projects effectively. You'll gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and techniques, and develop the leadership skills necessary to guide cross-functional teams to project success. Whether you're an aspiring project manager or a professional looking to enhance your project management skills, this course will prepare you for the challenges of managing complex tech projects.

Duration 10 weeks
Skill Level Beginner to Intermediate
Prerequisites Basic understanding of business processes
Certificate OKV Technology Project Management Certification

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Project Management

  • Overview of project management concepts and methodologies
  • The project life cycle and project management processes
  • Role of the project manager and key stakeholders
  • Project management in the context of technology and software development

Module 2: Project Initiation and Planning

  • Developing project charters and scope statements
  • Stakeholder analysis and management
  • Creating work breakdown structures (WBS)
  • Time and cost estimation techniques

Module 3: Agile Project Management

  • Agile principles and methodologies (Scrum, Kanban, XP)
  • Sprint planning and backlog management
  • Agile estimation and velocity tracking
  • Scaling agile for larger projects

Module 4: Risk Management and Quality Assurance

  • Identifying and assessing project risks
  • Developing risk response strategies
  • Quality planning and control in tech projects
  • Implementing continuous improvement processes

Module 5: Project Execution, Monitoring, and Control

  • Leading and managing project teams
  • Communication strategies for project success
  • Performance tracking and earned value management
  • Change management and scope control
