Extended Reality (XR) Course

Explore the future of immersive technologies: VR, AR, and MR

Course Overview

Our Extended Reality (XR) course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of the exciting world of immersive technologies. This course covers Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR), collectively known as Extended Reality or XR.

Throughout this course, you'll learn the fundamentals of XR technologies, gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and platforms, and develop the skills to create immersive experiences across various industries. Whether you're a developer, designer, or a tech enthusiast, this course will prepare you for the rapidly growing field of XR.

Duration 12 weeks
Skill Level Intermediate to Advanced
Prerequisites Basic programming and 3D graphics knowledge
Certificate OKV Technology XR Specialist Certification

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Extended Reality

  • Overview of VR, AR, and MR technologies
  • History and evolution of XR
  • XR applications across industries
  • Current market trends and future prospects

Module 2: Virtual Reality (VR) Development

  • VR hardware and software platforms
  • 3D modeling and texturing for VR
  • Unity and Unreal Engine for VR development
  • Creating interactive VR experiences

Module 3: Augmented Reality (AR) Development

  • AR hardware and software platforms
  • Computer vision and image recognition for AR
  • ARCore and ARKit development
  • Creating mobile AR applications

Module 4: Mixed Reality (MR) and Spatial Computing

  • MR hardware and platforms (HoloLens, Magic Leap)
  • Spatial mapping and understanding
  • Gesture and voice interaction in MR
  • Developing applications for MR devices

Module 5: XR User Experience and Design

  • UX principles for immersive technologies
  • 3D interface design and spatial UI
  • Accessibility considerations in XR
  • Performance optimization for XR applications
